Welcome to Loving Homes

HongKong' Premier Luxury Hotel for Dogs

Your Dog's Home Away From Home

At Loving Homes, we understand that your furry friend is more than just a pet — they are a cherished member of your family. That’s why we provide a safe, fun, and luxurious environment designed especially for their comfort and happiness while you’re away. From our state-of-the-art facilities to our caring and fully trained staff, we guarantee a tail-wagging good time for every dog that stays with us. Whether your pup loves playtime, enjoys calm relaxation, or prefers extra cuddles, we make sure their individual needs are met with love and attention. Our hotel offers spacious suites, regular exercise sessions, premium meals, and plenty of enrichment activities to keep them healthy, stimulated, and happy throughout their stay. At Loving Homes, your dog’s wellbeing is our top priority, and we are committed to creating a trusted, homely, and enjoyable experience that gives owners complete peace of mind.
